Identifying how to publish cardiology research in high-impact journals in the UAE effectively hinges on bypassing these administrative friction points. Cardiology Sub-specialties We Support for Publication

Cardiology is highly fragmented. A standard medical writer cannot adequately draft a complex interventional manuscript. We provide specialized medical writing for cardiologists across the UAE, spanning several discrete cardiovascular sub-disciplines:
